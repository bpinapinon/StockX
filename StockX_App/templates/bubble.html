Visualization of Price Premium and Popularity by Color

For this visualization, color was investigated. The chart below shows the relationship of the color of sneaker with the average retail price, average sale price, and average number of sales, which is set as the scaled radius of each bubble.

Analysis

Price Premium

Although purple Nike sneakers have a lower average retail price than the other colors investigated, it has a much higher resell price. This high price premium may be due to the rarity and desirability of the classic colorway.

Nike
Jordan 1 Retro High Court Purple

Popularity

The eye popping pink Adidas sneakers are the most popular, based on average number of sales, on StockX. While they do not have a high price premium, they are still sold for higher than retail price in large quantities.
